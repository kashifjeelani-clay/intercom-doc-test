# Trigify integration

Find prospects on social media.

Trigify is a social media monitoring tool.

This integration helps you find prospects who engage with specific keywords or topics on social media.

## **Find social prospects with Trigify**

1.  In your workspace, click `Create new` → `Table...`.
2.  Search for `Trigify` and select `Find prospects engaging with social content with Trigify`.
3.  Add your own account or use the Clay-managed account.
4.  Enter inputs for:
    -   **Post keywords:** Keywords that prospects have engaged with.
        -   Separate multiple keywords with commas. The integration finds prospects who engaged with posts containing ANY of these keywords (OR logic).
        -   Example: Entering "marketing automation, email marketing" finds prospects who engaged with posts about either topic.
    -   **Number of days since engagement**
    -   **Prospect job titles (Optional)**
    -   **Prospect job titles to exclude (Optional)**
    -   **Prospect company domains (Optional)**
    -   **Prospect company domains to exclude (Optional)**
    -   **Prospect industries (Optional)**
    -   **Prospect industries to exclude (Optional)**
    -   **Max number of results**
    -   **Additional search filters (Optional):**
        -   Prospect company sizes
        -   Prospect company sizes to exclude
        -   Prospect locations
        -   Prospect locations to exclude
        -   Prospect follower count
