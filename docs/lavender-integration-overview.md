# Lavender integration overview

AI email coach enhances writing.

### What is Lavender?

Lavender’s email rating tool provides a quality score for your emails, helping you gauge their effectiveness at a glance.

Aim for a score of 90 or above to increase your chances of a response.

### How do you use Lavender?

To use Lavender, simply access it through the enrichment panel. Each enriched cell will automatically use 1 credit, managed directly through your Clay account.

**Step 1: Select the Rate your emails action**

You can access Lavender from the enrichment search bar.

**Step 2: Input the email components**

Input the email body and email subject to recieve the score of the email.

**Step 3: Configure run settings**

Auto-update: Lavender will automatically enrich any new rows that get added to the table. Learn more about auto-update in this [this brief guide](https://docs.clay.com/en/articles/9642165-auto-update-and-auto-dedupe-table).

Conditional runs: To run enrichment only under specific conditions, use formulas that trigger the column when the formula is true. See [this Clay University lesson](<https://www.clay.com/university/lesson/ai-formulas-conditional-runs-clay-101#:~:text=Conditional runs \(which make use,personal emails for all rows\).\)>).

**Step 4: Choose data to add as columns to table**

Select which data from the enrichment you’d like to add as columns to your table. Even if you choose not to add columns at this point, the enriched data will still be available and accessible for later use.
