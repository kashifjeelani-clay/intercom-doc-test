# Find AI integration

Find people and companies with natural language.

## Overview

[UseFind.ai](http://usefind.ai/) is an AI-powered search engine that helps businesses find specific people or companies by scanning millions of profiles based on simple, natural language queries. It eliminates the need for complex filters, making it easy to generate precise lead lists.

For example, you can find potential clients by searching for roles like “Heads of Marketing at SaaS startups” or “CFOs at manufacturing companies.”

## How Usefind.ai works

**Natural Language Queries**: Enter queries like “Angels in B2B SaaS” or “CIOs in small family offices.”

**Match Types**:

-   **Exact**: Results meet all criteria.
-   **Partial**: Results meet some criteria for a broader list.

**Set Results Limit**: Control match volume (1 Clay Credit per result). Ex. If you request up to 100 matches and only 90 are found, you’ll be billed for 90 credits.

**Pricing:** Use your Clay Credits to access Find AI, with a cost of 1 credit per match

## How to use Usefind.ai

To use Find people or Companies with Find AI:

**Step 1:** In a workbook, click `+ Add` at the bottom. Search for `Find AI` and select from the results.

**Step 2:** Enter Your Query

Type in your natural language question, like “AI startups in NYC”, or “Engineering managers at technology companies who work on search infrastructure”.

**Step 3:** Set Maximum Results

Specify the maximum number of results you wish to receive.

**Step 4:** Choose Match Type

Select whether you want only exact matches.

**Step 5: Review and Use Results**

Run the search and the matching leads will populate in your sheet.
