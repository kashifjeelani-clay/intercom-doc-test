# Find your ICP based on your domain

Use your company website to find your ICP.

_Note: This entire enrichment costs entire 2 credits for the entire search._

### Overview

The **Find your ICP based on your Domain** source automatically fills your search criteria for **Find Company**. Pulls data from your website to instantly finding relevant accounts.

See [Find Company documentation](http://www.clay.com/university/lesson/find-company-overview) for more information on the Find Company search source.

### How to use Find your ICP?

**Step 1: Choose Find your ICP based on your Domain**

From the source panel you select **Find your ICP.**

**Step 2: Enter your website URL**

Clay will help find target companies via the Find Company search based off your website.

**Step 3: Verify the companies in your list and preview**

Preview the list and adjust any filters that were automatically selected from your website’s information.

**Step 4: Import companies into a new or existing new table**

If you’re adding to an existing table, note that auto-update may enrich new rows automatically.
