# Template of the month

Publish your Clay template to be considered for a prize

## 🖼️ What is Clay's "Template Of The Month" competition?

There are thousands of ways to use Clay.

Every month GTM operators share their most creative Clay use cases. Now, you can be rewarded for your own creativity.

Starting in November 2024, we're excited to introduce our new "Template Of The Month" competition: **Whoever submits the most popular template to** [**clay.com/templates**](https://www.clay.com/templates) **will be rewarded 3,000 bonus Clay credits**.

Let the games begin.

## 🏆 How to Win

It's easy as 1-2-3.

1.  **Submit your template** – Proud of what you built with Clay and believe others would benefit? [Submit it here](https://clayhq.com/to/uUg5NF7g)
2.  **Promote your template** - Share what you built with others to boost the popularity.
3.  **Win 3,000 bonus Clay credits** - You'll win 3,000 bonus credits if you win "Template of The Month"

You'll get an email from Clay team directly if you win. Results are shared in the Clay newsletter every month.

## 🧐 Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can the same template win more than one month?** 

No. For example, if you win Template Of The Month in November 2024 it's not eligible to win a second time. You will only be rewarded 3,000 bonus Clay credits one time.

**Q: Can I win more than one time if I submit multiple templates?** 

Yes. For example, if you win Template Of The Month in November 2024 and also win in December 2024, you'll be eligible to get bonus credits again. One person can win multiple times. However, only one template can win one time.
