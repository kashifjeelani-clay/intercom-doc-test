# Google Shopping integration overview

Discover and compare products across various retailers.

## Google Shopping Overview

Google Shopping lets you search for product details like prices, availability, and merchant info. With the Google Shopping integration in Clay, you can quickly access up-to-date shopping data for enhanced e-commerce insights and market research.

## **Available Actions with the Google Shopping Integration**

### `Action` **Find Shopping Results**

Use this action to query products on Google Shopping based on a specific search term and retrieve comprehensive results.

**Setup Inputs**

-   **Query**: Enter or select a column containing the search term or product you want to find.
-   **Include Featured, Inline, and Related Results** (Optional): Enable this option to include featured, inline, and related results along with standard search results.
-   **Language Localization** (Optional): Specify the language for the search. Defaults to English (en).
