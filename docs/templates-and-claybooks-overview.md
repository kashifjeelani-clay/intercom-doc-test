# Templates and Claybooks

Understand Clay use cases with Templates and Claybooks.

Whether you're exploring Clay for the first time or looking to streamline your workflows, **Claybooks** and **Templates** are the perfect starting points to help you set up workflows for making the most out of Clay.

### **Claybooks**

Claybooks showcase what's possible with Clay through interactive, step-by-step guides. Each Claybook highlights a specific use case, such as automating QA for support tickets, enabling you to quickly recreate workflows and achieve your goals.

Explore all available Claybooks at [clay.com/claybooks](http://clay.com/claybooks).

### **Templates**

Templates are ready-to-use starting points that help you implement common workflows in Clay. Simply copy a template, input your data, and run the table instantly. Explore all available templates at [clay.com/templates](https://clay.com/templates).
