# Enrichment recipes

Save your enrichment sequences and combinations with recipes.

_Important note: This feature is only available to enterprise customers._

## What are recipes?

Recipes are pre-built plug and play combinations of enrichments you can run.

## Use a recipe

To use a recipe:

1.  Select `Add enrichment` from your table to access the enrichment discovery panel.
2.  **Navigate** to the top menu of the enrichment discovery panel, click on `Recipes`, and select the recipe you want to run.
3.  **Navigate** to the top menu of the enrichment discovery panel,
4.  **Save** and/or **run** your enrichment.

## Create a recipe

You can save existing enrichment configurations as recipes. To create a recipe:

1.  Hold `Control` or `Command`, then select the columns you want to include in the recipe.
2.  **Right-click** on the highlighted columns and select `Create Recipe`.
3.  **Enter** a name, description, and inputs for your recipe.
4.  Select `Create recipe` to save your recipe.

## Delete a recipe

To delete your recipe:

1.  Select `Add enrichment` from your table to open the enrichment discovery panel.
2.  **Navigate** to the top menu of the enrichment discovery panel and click on `Recipes`.
3.  Click on the `...` menu to the right of the recipe you want to delete, then select **Delete**.
